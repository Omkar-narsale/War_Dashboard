# 🌍 Global Ripple Effects: How Conflict Impacts the World Economy

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive data visualization dashboard that analyzes how regional conflicts create ripple effects across the global economy. It focuses on understanding the cause-effect relationship between geopolitical tensions and key economic indicators such as oil prices, inflation, stock markets, and country-level impacts.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

A war in one region often disrupts global supply chains, energy prices, and inflation worldwide.

**Objective:**
To visualize how a major conflict impacts the global economy by identifying:

* Cause-effect relationships
* Most affected countries
* Winners vs losers
* Global economic patterns

---

## 📊 Key Features of the Dashboard

### 🔹 Oil Price Shock Analysis

Tracks oil price surges during conflict phases and highlights oil volatility as a key economic trigger.

### 🔹 Global Inflation Trends

Compares inflation rates across countries to show the ripple effect of rising energy costs.

### 🔹 Stock Market Reaction

Analyzes how financial markets respond to geopolitical instability.

### 🔹 Fuel Price Impact

Examines country-wise fuel price changes and energy dependency.

### 🔹 Winners vs Losers Analysis

Classifies countries based on economic impact into resilient and highly affected economies.

### 🔹 Global Impact Map

Visualizes the geographical spread of economic impact across countries.

---

## 🧠 Analytical Framework

**Conflict → Oil Price Shock → Inflation Rise → Market Reaction → Country Impact**

This structured approach helps uncover causal relationships rather than just trends.

---

## 📂 Datasets Used

* Crude Oil Price Data
* World Bank Inflation Data
* Stock Market Data
* Fuel Price Data
* Country Impact Dataset

---

## ⚠️ Data Considerations

* Analysis includes only countries with complete data across all indicators
* Some countries (e.g., Sri Lanka) were excluded due to missing data
* Commodity impacts are inferred using oil price trends as a proxy

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX

---

## 📈 Key Insight

> Oil price volatility serves as a leading indicator for broader commodity inflation and global economic stress.
